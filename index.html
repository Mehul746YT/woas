Introduction & Mission

Wolves of Alt Street is a chain-agnostic cryptocurrency educational Discord community that focuses on self-custodianship and spreading basic crypto knowledge and best security practices.

The server has platformed 30 projects since 2021, including projects such as ShapeShift.

Necksus and the Wolves community provide critical feedback and focus-test style engagement to Web3 projects.

The community works to evaluate project needs and deliver value to the Web3 ecosystem.

Under Necksus’ watch, Wolves of Alt Street has given away tens of thousands of dollars in cryptocurrency and helped many Discord users safely begin their DeFi journey.
